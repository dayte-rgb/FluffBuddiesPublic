# Interview Notes: David

## Press Conference Notes

- Banjo is lazy, wants to hire other pets do their work for them
- Kazooie is more into the business side, how she can hire her services to others, she is good at hunting. They are not very clear what they want entirely, want us to help them decide what the priorities are
- Successful Interaction: Want a proper profile, or pets, can pick who they are, to have some different categories of pets, have a profile where they can post jobs and hire others. Would be happy if they can rank profiles, or a rating system, 5 paws if good, and also a comment with that or a commenting system. They are not sure how the jobs should be displayed, between like Craigslist or Instagram, but they want it to be searchable. They want a dog who does X job and have 4+ stars and have completed these tasks. They want a scheduling system to prevent double hiring. 
- Pet vs Owner: You can have pets and owner, unsure if we want the owner to have an admin role, so some pets need the owners to do translations possibly, but the power dynamic is likely in question
- Communication: Very concerned about privacy, worried that their paws might not be good for texting, so ideally it would be good to have a messaging system in the website, but it might be too difficult, phone number listed, when clicked, goes to an app for texting or directly calls. But integrated communication system is preferred. 
- App vs. Website: Preferred if it is an app due to accessibility that some pets might not have phones. Some advanced websites can actually work as apps too, like Burger King, just slightly differently presented. 
- Animals in Shelters: Can assign multiple pets to one human, so the humans can take care of the scheduling and transportation for them. 
- Skill Validation: No limitations, can be good however to list certificates, degrees maybe too (my idea), on the website that appears as a star or some symbol to indicate that. 
- Retention of People: Likely a secondary project, but you could implement a leaderboard or point based reward system. 
- Included in the Profile: Names, species, a picture or possibly create your own avatar. Show skills, specialties, pronouns, show their schedule for booking, shows their ratings and their reviews. Maybe you can win tag-lines “Master of Gambling” for example,  for your tag name for more creativity / retention system. 
- Reviews: Shouldn’t allow everyone to comment, so there should be a way to verify that the completed the job, this can be for example sharing a private code between the employee and the requester, so just put some control so that bots cannot comment. 
- Additional Verification: Login page, enter user information, 2 way verification, possible think about entering a credit card - they expect the login page with username and password and a way to reset the password - in the database, they just want to make sure that its secure
- Rating System: Should it be one way rating system or a mutual rating system, they would be interested in the mutual rating system and would be open to that 
- Limit to Owner Pet Dependencies: No limit to the number of pets an owner can have
- Shelter/Organization vs Individual: Can have a verified badge or star that verifies that this organization is real, or cruelty free, or a no-kill shelter. Think about 3 types of users, pets, humans, organizations.
